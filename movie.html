3. Zindagi Na Milegi Doobara

This movie is three friends who plans a trip to spain before the marriage of one of the friend.This trip ultimately turns into an opportunity for the friends to heal wounds and fall in love with the life again with new energy.

This movie shows one of the great festival of spain i.e, 'The Tomatina Festival' which is actually the tomato fight.it also represents some adventure.

It is been said that after this movie the spanish economy had a great raise in the field of sports.
